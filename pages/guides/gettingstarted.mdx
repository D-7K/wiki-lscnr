# LSCNR - Los Santos Cops and Robbers

![LSCNR Banner](https://lscnr.net/assets/logo.png)

## Introduction

So, you just flew into the city, but still finding your way around? No worries, this guide will help you figure out how to get started. This guide will cover the basics, and most of the relevant information for a beginner.

## Objectives

LSCNR is an open city, the main goals are those you set yourself. Want to become a kingpin, driving luxury cars across the city, while feared throughout the underworld? Go right ahead, just beware its a long journey. Want to become a legendary officer, the bane of all criminals throughout? Make your way up there, and earn the place. There is a place for everyone in LSCNR, you just need to find yours.

## Important details

LSCNR is not a roleplay server. Roleplay is allowed, and depending on the person, sometimes even encouraged, but it is not a requirement. Make sure to read the rules, to guarantee the best experience both for you, and all other players.

## The teams

LSCNR is primarily divided into three teams, but there are many jobs available that subdivide those teams. For now, we will focus on the three main teams for simplicity.

- **Criminals**
  - Cause chaos and do crime around the city. Maybe for money, maybe for power, maybe for something else entirely. As a criminal, you have many options, from small time mugging and substance selling, all the way to bank heists and exporting most wanted cars to the mafia. Widely considered the best team for beginners, due to the minimal requirements and simplicity in early play.
- **Civillians**
  - Tired of the constant fighting? Settle down and start a legitimate life. Choose from a variety of legal jobs, and help keep the city running. There are plenty of options, from pizza deliveries and taxi driving all the way to long range cargo transport. These options are often more progression based, and take more time to get into, but pay out nontheless. These are also good options for new citizens.
- **Police**
  - Enforce the law, and keep crime at bay. This team requires **level 9** to join, due to its increased difficulty. Go around stopping members of the Criminal team, making arrests, and even confiscating illegal items. This job is not reccomended for new citizens, due to the level requirement, and high difficulty.

## Frequently asked questions
- **Who can I attack?**
  - You can attack anyone in a red zone. Outside of a red zone, you can only attacl Criminals (as a criminal or a cop) as long as you have a justification, and Police, if playing as a Criminal. Both criminals and police may only attack civillian players if they are posing a threat.
- **What are the requirements for changing teams**
  - You don't have to stick to any one team! As long as you aren't wanted, you can switch between Civillian and Police whenever you want, and you can switch between Civillian and Criminal by gaining/losing Wanted level.
- **Why can't I buy cars?**
  - You can, you just need a garage. Only garages you have enough levels for are visible on the map. If you can't see any, chances are you are too low a level.
- **Someone broke a rule, how do I report it**
  - Make a ticket or call an admin. You can see which admins are online by typing /admins in chat
