# LSCNR - Los Santos Cops and Robbers

![LSCNR Banner](https://lscnr.net/assets/logo.png)

## Introduction

So, you wanna do crime. Fair choice. Here is what you need to know

## Wanted levels

These display how wanted you are by Police, and often align with how much they will attempt to arrest you. They will be gained for each crime you commit, depending on the severity, and slowly lower over time under specific circumstances. There are 5 Wanted levels, upon reaching level 5, you will become most wanted and gain a bounty.

## Most wanted

Upon reaching most wanted status, you will gain a bounty, which will increase for each crime you commit, and slowly lower over time under specific circumstances. Your bounty can be claimed by any criminal/police that takes you down, or by any Police who arrest you. 

## Criminal Jobs

There are a variety of options for both new and experienced criminals, depending on their needs and preferences. For simplicity, this guide will separate them into 3 tiers, based on their complexity and requirements. The list is in order of difficulty.

- **Small time crime**
  - Pickpocketing
  - Parking meter theft
  - Car scrapping
  - Store holdups
- **Crime**
  - ATM hacking
  - Substance selling
  - Substance growing
  - House break-ins
  - Vehicle exporting
- **Grand crime**
  - Bank truck robberies
  - Bank robberies
  - Heists
  - Airdrop theft

## Where to start

It is reccomendable to start off with small-time crime, to earn the money for the requirements for larger, more profitable crimes, and move up from there. There are guides for each crime, explaining how to commit them, what is required, and what you can expect to gain.

## FAQs

- **How do I lose a wanted level**
  - If you aren't most wanted, you can instantly lose your wanted level by going to a police station and paying a fine. Alternatively, you can wait outside of the Search radius (blue circle on radar) and it will go down over time if you stop commiting crimes.
- **How do I lose most wanted status**
  - You can lose most wanted status by being arrested, or being taken down by any other criminal or police.
