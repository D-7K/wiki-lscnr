## Introduction

So, you wish to be an upstanding citizen. Here, you will find the information you need.

## Progression

Some  civillian jobs (e.g trucking) have a progression system, and will give better payouts as you do more, and improve your skills. If a job isn't paying very well, chances are you need to progress more.

## Civillian Job Seletion

Civillian jobs can be split into two categories, low requirement jobs, and high requirement jobs. High requirement jobs might require a higher inital investment, or more progression, while low requirement jobs require much less to get started.

- **Low requirement jobs**
  - Trash rummaging
  - Lootbox finding
  - Pizza delivery
  - Taxi driving
- **High requirement Jobs**
  - Diving
  - Trucking

## Where to start

It is reccomendable to start off with easier, low requirement jobs, but if you want to spend your initial cash or can afford it, you can jump straight into some of the high requirement jobs. The choice is yours.
